About Palomar Health – San Diego

Palomar Health, located in North San Diego County, is one of California’s largest healthcare districts, dedicated to providing high-quality medical care and innovative health services to the community. With a network of advanced hospitals and healthcare facilities, Palomar Health leads in patient care, research, and public health initiatives.

Through community engagement, social media optimization, and digital innovation, Palomar Health continues to improve lives by connecting patients, professionals, and families with the resources they need most.
